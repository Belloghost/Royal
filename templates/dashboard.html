Oriki of the Royal Chuckles

Oriki(Eulogy)

Omo Soun logbomoso, Omo jogi oro Akande maso, Omo erun koje ndeyin Ogba nba fun e nisu je, Omo Oba meje ti mejeje si joba, Omo aduro gbofa bi ogiri, Omo ajoko tolowu Maran ofeso eso mowo redi Idi e, Olowu obinu Oko re lo so, Omo otoro obe toro Abe Nile baba tobi yin lomo.

Praise

We are the Royal Chuckles, descendants of the great Bello lineage. Our family is known for its wisdom, strength, and unity. We cherish our heritage and uphold the values passed down through generations.

Our ancestors were leaders in their communities, known for their generosity and wisdom. They taught us the importance of family, respect for elders, and the value of hard work.

We are the children of the lion, fearless and strong. We are the children of the eagle, with vision that sees far and wide. We are the children of the river, flowing with life and abundance.

In times of joy, we celebrate together. In times of challenge, we support each other. Our laughter is medicine, our bond unbreakable.

May our family continue to prosper and bring joy to all who know us. May we always remember who we are and where we come from. We are the Royal Chuckles - the pride of the Bello family.
